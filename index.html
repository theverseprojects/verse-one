The Story

When Jesus actually shows up

Ebun is 17, British-Nigerian, and deeply unimpressed with inherited faith. As a podcaster, he does what no one has dared — posts an open invitation for Jesus to come on the show.

Jesus arrives. In a leather jacket.

What follows is a chase through time and space — from a war-torn street in Syria to the silence of the South African veldt — ending in an otherworldly shearing barn where Ebun confronts the spiritual captivity he never knew he was in.

This is the gospel. Unfiltered. Multicultural. Impossible to ignore.
